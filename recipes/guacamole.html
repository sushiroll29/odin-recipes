Guacamole recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • Small onion, finely chopped
  • Ripe tomato, chopped
  • Garlic clove, minced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mash avocados in medium serving bowl.
  2. Stir in onion, tomato and garlic.
  3. Season with lime juice, salt and pepper.
  4. Cover and chill guacamole for 30 minutes to allow flavors to blend.
