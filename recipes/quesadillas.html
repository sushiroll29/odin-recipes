Quesadilla recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Shredded cooked chicken
  • Salsa
  • Green onions
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Dried oregano
  • Tortilla
  • Shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Combine the first 6 ingredients into a large skillet and cook, uncovered, over medium heat, for about 10 minutes.
  2. Brush one side of the tortilla with butter and place it buttered side down on a baking sheet.
  3. Spoon 1/3 cup chicken mixture over half of the tortilla; sprinkle with 1/3 cup cheese.
  4. Fold plain side of the tortilla over filling and cheese.
  5. Bake at 375 until tortilla is crisp and golden brown (9-11 minutes).
  6. Can be served with sour cream and guacamole.
